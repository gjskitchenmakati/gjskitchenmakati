The Owners

×

The business is owned and run by the Lipaygo couple. Jeffrey Lipaygo, also known as Pipoy, is in charge of buying the ingredients as well as delivering orders to their customers. On the other hand, Gebel Lipaygo, a graduate of BS in Hospitality Management Specializing in Culinary Arts and Technology at the Philippine Women University, facilitates the social media accounts of the business, plates and takes photos of their food for their online posts, and packs customer orders. Both also share the same work when it comes to food preparation and cooking.

G&J's Kitchen

×

G & J’s Kitchen aims to deliver on people’s expectations of what good food made with love tastes like. The company ensures that every bite of their snacks will satisfy and guarantee a smile on their customers’ faces. Furthermore, this kitchen aims to be a successful business that can cater to a wider audience so that more people can enjoy their delicious food
